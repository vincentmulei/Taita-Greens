Our Production Model

Taita Greens Ltd has adopted a hybrid approach which utilizes both our valued out-grower farmers, our own dedicated production farms, and certified suppliers for an assortment of fresh produce.

Out-Grower Network

Our network of out-growers is strategically distributed across several key counties, ensuring a diverse and consistent supply:

  • Taita-Taveta
  • Makueni
  • Machakos
  • Kajiado
  • Kirinyaga
  • Meru
  • Embu
  • Nyeri
  • Murang’a
  • Nakuru
  • Uasin Gishu
  • Bomet
  • Nyandarua

Own Production Farms

Our own farms are located in Makueni, Machakos, and Kirinyaga counties, with a cumulative acreage of 200 acres dedicated to our high-quality production.

Certified Suppliers

We also collaborate with a network of certified suppliers, ensuring all produce meets our stringent quality and safety standards.

Exceptional Services to Our Farmers

We believe that high-quality produce begins at the farm. To this end, Taita Greens Ltd offers exceptional support and services to our out-grower farmers:

Field Agronomist Guidance

Our experienced field agronomists are always available to guide farmers in their production activities, ensuring optimal growth and quality.

Timely Produce Collection

We ensure prompt and efficient collection of produce from farms, maintaining freshness and reducing post-harvest losses.

Competitive Prices

We offer competitive prices for farmers' produce, ensuring fair compensation for their hard work.

Timely Payments

Payments are made on time, fostering trust and strong, reliable partnerships with our out-growers.

Our Commitment to Quality Assurance

Taita Greens Ltd Company’s Quality Assurance policy is to always supply high quality produce that meets the customer’s requirements. We continuously invest and engage professionalism along all value chains relentlessly to assure quality.

Adherence to Global Food Safety Standards

In keeping with our quality policy, we employ and update our food safety standards accordingly, aligning with internationally recognized principles and certifications such as:

  • Globalgap
  • Fairtrade
  • BRC (British Retail Consortium)
  • HACCP principles (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points)
