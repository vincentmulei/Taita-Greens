Our Valued Workforce

Farm & Packhouse Teams

Our commitment to quality is upheld daily by our diligent casual labor force, ranging from 35 to 50 individuals. We are proud that 78% of these employees are women, who play a vital role at both the farm and packhouse levels, ensuring efficient operations and meticulous handling of our produce.

Their enthusiasm and commitment are key to the excellent quality and quantity of produce supplied to our clients abroad.

Our Culture: Teamwork & Motivation

At Taita Greens Ltd, we believe in team work and make sure that all staff stay motivated to do their work with enthusiasm and commitment. This inculcated spirit results in excellent quality and quantity produce that is supplied to our clients abroad; the reason why Taita Greens Ltd is gaining markets for her produce.

Our success is a testament to the collective effort and dedication of every individual in our team, from the leadership to the field and packhouse staff.
