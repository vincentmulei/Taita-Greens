About Taita Greens Ltd

Taita Greens Ltd is a Kenyan company incorporated in 2020, evolving from a local supplier to a leading exporter of fresh horticultural produce. With over 15 years of industry expertise from our dedicated professionals, we are committed to delivering high-quality products to markets in the Middle East, Holland, Canada, the United Kingdom, and France. We pride ourselves on a woman-led management, a competent team of agronomists, and a strong focus on quality from farm to market.
