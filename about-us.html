Our Story: Nurturing Growth, Delivering Excellence

Our Journey

Taita Greens Ltd is a Kenyan company incorporated within the Laws of the Republic of Kenya. The company started operations in Kenya in 2020 as a grower and supplier of local vegetables to local retail, wholesale, and supermarkets.

In an effort to expand the business, the company later engaged in growing Fresh Horticultural Produce meant for export markets as a supplier to Kenyan fresh produce exporting companies. Building on experience gained from production of Fresh fruits and vegetables for supply to exporting companies, Taita Greens Ltd expanded into fresh produce export operations and registered as Taita Greens Ltd 2021. The company is blessed with professionals having work experience of over 15 years within the Horticulture industry.

Leadership & Workforce

The company is Woman-led with three directors with wide experience in business management. Our team includes five (5) competent agronomists headed by a technical manager, all of whom have over ten (10) years professional experience in Agricultural value chains.

Taita Greens Ltd is also a significant local employer, engaging casual labour during working days in the range of 35 to 50 individuals, of which 78% are women at farm and packhouse level. We believe in empowering our local communities and providing sustainable employment opportunities.

Our Vision

To be the leading, trusted supplier of fresh produce of high quality, in required quantities and in a consistent manner.

Our Mission

To offer professional services to out-grower farmers, under the technical advice of motivated staff both at field and pack house level, so as to ensure supply of fresh produce to the preference of our customers.

Our Values

  • Real time delivery
  • Team work

  • Commitment

Location & Global Reach

The company has offices located off Airport North road, behind Taj Mall approximately 5 Km from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA). Currently, the company packs her produce utilizing the Horticultural Crops Directorate pack house located at the JKIA premises.

Our major markets for Taita Greens Ltd produce are the Middle East, Holland, Canada, United Kingdom and France, ensuring our fresh produce reaches discerning customers worldwide.

Corporate Social Responsibility

At Taita Greens Ltd, we hold in high esteem our farmers and their workers, our staff, the communities surrounding all our production and produce packing sites, as well as the natural resources that support our business.

It is for this reason that all our activities are carried out whilst observing internationally acceptable standards and practices that promote sustainability mechanisms. We understand that the application of such standards as Globalgap, BRC, and Fairtrade provides a framework for the sustainability of the environment, good health and safety of workers, and the well-being of our final customers, thereby promoting a truly responsible business model.

We are unwavering in our spirit of implementing and continuously updating these standards for the collective good of the environment, and all flora and fauna that thrive within it.

Future Growth & Performance

Taita Greens Ltd envisages field production, packing and exporting 50 tons of fresh vegetables per week from October 2025 onwards. Building on this momentum, we plan to introduce fruits exports by March 2026, further expanding our product portfolio and market presence.

This ambitious growth is supported by our robust operations and dedicated team, ensuring we meet global demand for high-quality produce.
